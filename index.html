Packing Suggestions

It's not worth having a list if you forget to put something on it. That's why Travel Checklist has packing suggestions! When you're creating your list, Travel Checklist will show you some commonly packed vacation items, and you can simply tap them to add them to your list. So you'll never forget things like phone chargers and medication. Plus, your suggestions automatically adjust based on what you're doing on your trip. So if you tell Travel Checklist you're going swimming, it'll add swimsuits and towels to your list. And for the items that Travel Checklist can't think of (which is very few), you can dictate them directly to your list with the link on the Dasboard. We've taken typing out of list-making. You're welcome.
